My Expertise

AI: Shaping the Future of AI in Industry and Innovation I have extensive experience in applied AI , driving transformative change across industries, from innovative startups to award-winning projects. My work spans a wide range of AI applications, with a focus on improving efficiency, sustainability, and innovation in complex environments. Current Role : Leading embedded AI development in an industry startup, enhancing industrial processes through advanced AI integration. Notable Project : As part of an interdisciplinary team, I pioneered the first automatic vegetation detection system for train tracks at Deutsche Bahn — a project recognized with the German AI Innovation Award by the Federal AI Association (KI Bundesverband ). Prior experience includes AI product development as CTO and co-founder of a startup that automated social media platforms . Significant contributions to AI research during my tenure at the Fraunhofer Institute for AI and Birds on Mars , an AI-driven consultancy.
