Areas of Expertise

AI Solutions

Shaping the Future of AI in Industry and Innovation I have extensive experience in applied AI , driving transformative change across industries, from innovative startups to award-winning projects. My work spans a wide range of AI applications, with a focus on improving efficiency, sustainability, and innovation in complex environments. Current Role : Leading embedded AI development in an industry startup, enhancing industrial processes through advanced AI integration. Notable Project : As part of an interdisciplinary team, I pioneered the first automatic vegetation detection system for train tracks at Deutsche Bahn — a project recognized with the German AI Innovation Award by the Federal AI Association (KI Bundesverband ). Prior experience includes AI product development as CTO and co-founder of a startup that automated social media platforms . Significant contributions to AI research during my tenure at the Fraunhofer Institute for AI and Birds on Mars , an AI-driven consultancy.

Solarpunk and Sustainable Tech

Pioneering Sustainable Futures with Solarpunk Vision & Technological Innovation As a passionate advocate for creating a better, sustainable world, I combine my technical expertise with a deeply rooted commitment to environmental causes. Through various ventures and volunteer work, I aim to foster tangible, eco-friendly innovation and creative solutions for climate action. Founder of a Solarpunk Think & Do Tank , dedicated to exploring and promoting sustainable transformation with futuristic, yet practical approaches. AI for Sustainability : Key contributor to innovative, AI-powered environmental projects, including the Deutsche Bahn train project , enhancing eco-friendly railway operations. Community Engagement : For over eleven years, I have actively volunteered to support climate policy development and educational outreach. Mentorship : I regularly mentor at youth hackathons , empowering the next generation to utilize technology to solve real-world sustainability challenges.

Quantum Computing

Education : Studied Quantum Computing at the Excellence University of Bonn , specializing in foundational quantum mechanics and computational applications.
